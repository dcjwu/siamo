What we offer

We offer our customers to participate in 3 different event formats followed by photo shooting, video recording and amazing atmosphere for all the partecipants:

Siamo Cyber

Physical players at the events and virtual players to follow our live streaming with a possibility to be a game changers of the same tournament, at the same time.

Siamo Friendly

Free of charge events which are made for anyone who wants to play football for fun without any pressure.

One Day Tournaments

An event with a sign-up fee played by the tournament rules with a group stage and play-offs where the winner is getting the prize.

Football Festival

Big football tournament with a sign-up fee where teams are competing for great prizes and glory!

Siamo Street

In Siamo Street we are always trying to build a strong sense of sportsmanship between all members of cummunity by showing it through our events. This is the place where people need to feel good about themselves and to respect other members. Also, a big part of organising our events is to provide good and fun time for everyone that attends any of our event formats.

Siamo Street has got an honour to win Danish National Championship in Entrepreneurship 2020 organised by "Danish Foundation of Entrepreneurship" within the "Social Entrepreneurship" category.
