Siamo Street

Siamo Street is an urban-sport organization that exists with a purpose to grow a friendly and inclusive multicultural community that welcomes anyone regardless of skills,background or any other factors by promoting healthy and entertaining events of different formats in different street-sports.

Goals

Help internationals integrate into Danish community through street-sports.
Minimize negative ‘’street’’ impact in urban areas.
Provide a healthy and accessible platform for people to socialise.

We are Siamo

So what does Siamo mean? Everyone is having the same question except for Italins (haha). And we are revealing our secret, because in translation from Italian ‘’Si amo’’ means ‘’We are’’ and that makes ‘’Siamo Street’’ mean ‘’We Are Street’’ and we are here to make street-sports accessible for everyone. The whole project was started as a Bachelor project by 3 truly passionate sports students of the Innovation and Entrepreneurship programme from Aarhus Business Academy back in June 2020 and it is exciting to see how far we can get!

What do we stand for?

Respect

In Siamo Street there is only one main rule that community members must follow and it is to treat others the way you want to be treated.

Health

Sport and healthy lifestyle are two very important values of our community that we want to promote across younger generations.

Happiness

Being happy and feeling good about yourself is the emotion that we are always trying to project on others and make everyone around feel good and safe.

Inclusion

There is always a place for everyone no matter nationality, gender, age, culture, background, skills or any other factors. Street-sports are for everyone.

Meet the Team

Strength of our team is an outcome of great understanding and sense of respect between each team member. Everyone has a clear understanding of their tasks which makes the overall performance of the team much better.
